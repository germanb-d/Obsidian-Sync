# TP de Netflix 

a. Thumbnails are customized for each Netflix user because they want to grab your attention 

b. **AVA takes place in two stages: first**,the system analyzes every frame from a show and creates metadata for each frame **and then** the metadata is used to group the images into different types of shots based on composition, characters, and the quality of the picture.

c. Some examples of the data that Netflix collects about its users are the genres of shows and movies you watch, how much time you spend watching, where in the world you watch, and much more.

d. Nowadays, thumbnails keep changing on a daily basis due to your previous viewing history and your real-time interactions with the site.
