## Punto 1

**Write 2 questions that can be answered with the information from the text.**
Escribe en 2 preguntas que puedan ser respondidas con la imformación del texto.

1. What is the GUIs technology used to provide a user interface?
2. For what is used an mouse?

> [!note] Corrección
> ❌ "What is the GUIs technology used to provide a user interface?"
>
> "GUIs" es plural, pero usas "is" (singular)
> Falta artículo antes de "technology"
>
> **Correcciones posibles**:
> ✅ "What are the GUI technologies used to provide a user interface?"
> ✅ "What technology do GUIs use to provide the user interface?"
> ✅ "What is the most common GUI technology used to provide a user interface?"
>
> ❌ "For what is used an mouse?"
>
> Orden incorrecto de palabras
> "An mouse" → "a mouse" (mouse empieza con consonante)
>
> Correcciones posibles:
> ✅ "What is a mouse used for?"
> ✅ "For what is a mouse used?" (más formal pero menos natural)
