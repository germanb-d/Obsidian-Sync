# **1. Cómo Responder a "What is This Text about?"**

El objetivo es dar una **visión general y concisa** en una oración.

**Estructura básica:**
**"The text is about... / This text explains... / This text discusses..."** + [Tema principal] + [Propósito o contexto].

**Ejemplos de respuestas correctas:**

*   **Para un texto sobre SSDs:**
    *   "The text is about **Solid State Drives (SSDs), explaining what they are and how they differ from traditional hard drives**."
    *   "This text explains **the technology behind SSDs and why they are faster and more reliable than HDDs**."
*   **Para un texto sobre VPNs:**
    *   "The text is about **Virtual Private Networks (VPNs), describing how they work to protect online privacy and security**."
    *   "This text discusses **the function of VPNs in encrypting internet connections and hiding users' IP addresses**."
*   **Para un texto sobre un proceso (ej: la fotosíntesis):**
    *   "The text is about **the process of photosynthesis, detailing how plants convert light energy into chemical energy**."

**Qué evitar:**
*   **Demasiado detalle:** No menciones datos específicos o ejemplos secundarios.
*   **Demasiado vago:** "The text is about computers." (Es poco específico).

# **2. Cómo Responder a "Explain in 2-3 sentences"**

El objetivo es **sintetizar** ideas clave del texto, mostrando comprensión.

**Estructura básica:**
1.  **Oración 1:** Presenta la idea principal.
2.  **Oración 2:** Añade un detalle importante, una causa, una consecuencia o una comparación.
3.  **Oración 3 (opcional):** Otro detalle clave o una conclusión breve.

**Ejemplos de respuestas correctas:**

*   **Tema: ¿Por qué los SSDs son más rápidos que los HDDs?**
    1.  "Solid State Drives (SSDs) are faster than Hard Disk Drives (HDDs) because they have no moving parts."
    2.  "While HDDs use a mechanical arm to read data from a spinning platter, SSDs use flash memory, which allows for instant data access."
    3.  "This lack of physical movement makes SSDs more reliable, durable, and significantly quicker."

*   **Tema: ¿Cómo protege tu privacidad una VPN?**
    1.  "A VPN protects your privacy by creating an encrypted tunnel for your internet traffic."
    2.  "This encryption prevents hackers on public Wi-Fi from seeing your data, and it also hides your real IP address from the websites you visit."
    3.  "As a result, your online actions are much more secure and anonymous."

*   **Tema: La función del sistema operativo.**
    1.  "The operating system (OS) is the most important software that manages all the computer's hardware and software resources."
    2.  "It provides a user interface for people to interact with the computer and allows applications to run on the hardware."
    3.  "For example, it controls the CPU, memory, and storage, ensuring that different programs can function without conflict."
